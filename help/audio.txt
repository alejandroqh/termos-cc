AUDIO SETTINGS HELP

Control audio output, input, and per-application volume.

VOLUME CONTROL
--------------
Adjust master volume with quick presets or custom values.
Toggle mute on/off. Volume can go up to 150%.

OUTPUT DEVICE
-------------
Select default audio output (speakers, headphones, HDMI).
The [DEFAULT] marker shows current output device.

INPUT (MICROPHONE)
------------------
Control microphone volume and mute status.
Select input device for recording/calls.
Note: .monitor devices are excluded (internal use).

PER-APPLICATION VOLUME
----------------------
Adjust volume for individual applications currently
playing audio. Useful for balancing music and game
sounds, or muting specific apps.

Note: Only apps actively playing audio appear here.

AUDIO PROFILES
--------------
Switch between audio profiles (analog stereo, HDMI,
headset, etc.). Profiles depend on your sound card.

QUICK ACTIONS
-------------
Quick volume adjustment without entering submenus:
  - Increase (+10%)
  - Decrease (-10%)
  - Toggle Mute

ADVANCED
--------
For more control, open pulsemixer for a full-featured
terminal audio mixer.

DEPENDENCIES
------------
Required: pactl (PulseAudio/PipeWire)
Optional: pulsemixer (advanced mixer)
